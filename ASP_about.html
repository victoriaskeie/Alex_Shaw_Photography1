About Me

To say I take photos every once and a while would be an understatement. I love people, travelling and all things nature, so capturing them is one of my most favourite things.

I am a native panhandle Floridian, currently based in St Andrews, Scotland. Portrait photography and natural light are my forte and photoshop is my closest companion. I’m continually inspired by the people around me and the environment I’m in at the time, always ready to capture memories at any moment!
